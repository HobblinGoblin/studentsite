Who We Are

Starting a business? Want to promote your band? Looking to get a custom digital painting for that friend who has everything?

Her Savage Design is a boutique graphic design service in Hilo, Hawaii, that offers small businesses, non-profits and creatives an opportunity to get ahead of the curve with striking visual content and direct, focused marketing strategies. Logos, branding, web and print advertisements, signage and illustration are just the beginning. If you can dream it, we can recreate your vision. If you don’t have a vision, we will dream with you and create it together. Every client is exquisitely cared for throughout the design process. From brainstorming sessions to final hand offs: communication, quality and timeliness define us. We realize how digital media and business branding are of utmost importance in today’s market and we do not take this responsibility lightly. When you work with our designers you will have an enjoyable experience where your opinions and needs are valued. By working together we will create stunning graphic content to elevate your project to the next level. We love our community. We want to help local businesses succeed!

“After working with Andrea at Her Savage Design, our online exposure doubled and we have had 6 months of consecutive increases in sales.”
